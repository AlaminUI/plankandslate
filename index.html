Frequently asked questions

Flooring & tiling advice

Why choose LVP/LVT flooring?
LVP and LVT offer exceptional durability, water resistance, and realistic wood/stone appearances at competitive prices. Perfect for both residential and commercial applications.
What sets our moisture mitigation apart?
Our proprietary moisture prevention system creates an impermeable barrier that protects your flooring investment from subfloor moisture issues, preventing warping, buckling, and mold growth.
How long does installation take?
Most residential installations can be completed in 2-5 days, including our moisture mitigation process. Commercial projects are quoted based on square footage.
What preparation is needed?
We handle all aspects of floor preparation, including moisture testing, subfloor leveling, and application of our moisture mitigation system.
